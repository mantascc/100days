# What
Sound driving image directly: mic RMS and FFT bins mapped onto motion, force, glyph density, and phase-space. The maker calls it "direct sonification" — the ambition is for the visual to be a true read-out of the signal, not a meter dressed up as art. Five sketches deep, the technique is solid (analyser nodes, smoothing, band-splitting) but the aesthetic question of *inevitability* remains the live frontier.

# Charge
Reliably exciting to build, but shadowed by the gimmick tension — the work is best when the motion is a *consequence of optics*, not a reaction bolted on.

# Prompts
- Audio that controls a *rule*, not a value — sound changing the system's law.
- A piece where silence is as expressive as sound: the negative space of the signal.
- Cross audio with emergence: flocking whose alignment strength is the bass.

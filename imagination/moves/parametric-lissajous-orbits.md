# What
Closed-form parametric motion: cos/sin paths, rotated ellipses, Lissajous figures from mutually detuned frequencies. No integration, no state — just an angle driven by time and a pair of trig functions. It's the cheapest possible way to get organic, looping, never-quite-repeating motion, and it shows up wherever the maker wants idle, ambient movement without the cost of a full simulation.

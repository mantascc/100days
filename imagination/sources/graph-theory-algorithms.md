# What
The classical toolkit that makes the networks vein rigorous rather than decorative: Havel-Hakimi for realising a degree sequence into an actual graph, Kruskal with Union-Find for minimum spanning trees, planarity tests for crossing-free embeddings. These aren't just utilities — they shape what the graph sketches *can be*, turning "draw some nodes and edges" into "generate a graph that provably has this property." The structure comes from the algorithm, and the algorithm is the source.

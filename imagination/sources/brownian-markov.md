# What
The mathematics of memoryless motion. A Markov process depends only on its current state, not its history; the Wiener process (Brownian motion) is its continuous form, a random walk in the limit of infinitesimal steps. From these come the random walk on a lattice, the Brownian tree formed by diffusion-limited aggregation, and fBm's fractal roughness. This is the source that legitimises randomness as a *material* — it gives the practice's noise a rigorous backbone rather than mere arbitrariness.

Anna Smeragliuolo
Design Technologist

Moved from Sweden to the Bay Area. Works at the intersection of hardware, space, and interaction — installations, spatial systems, physical interfaces.

Drawn to concrete and the thresholds between materials. Keeps returning to rooms that ask something of the people inside them. Interested in what a design makes possible, not what it looks like.

Selected clients

IKEA. Mercedes-Benz. McDonald's. Above.
